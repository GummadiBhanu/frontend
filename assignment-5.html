The Attack: A Detailed Account

The militants approached tourists in the Baisaran Valley, surrounded by dense pine forests. They separated men from women and children, demanding that the men recite the Islamic kalima. Those who failed or were identified as non-Muslims were executed on the spot. Among the victims were newlywed couples, with men shot point-blank in front of their wives. A local pony ride operator, Adil Hussain, attempted to disarm one of the attackers but was killed in the process. His brother, Naushad Hussain, later recounted the tragedy amid growing rumors and social media coverag

Impact on Tourism and Local Economy

The attack had a significant impact on tourism in Kashmir. Many tourists canceled their trips, leading to a decline in business for local vendors and hospitality services. Despite the tragedy, some tourists reported that locals continued to extend a warm welcome, urging others not to cancel their visits due to fear or misinformation. Locals emphasized their commitment to ensuring the safety and comfort of visitors.
